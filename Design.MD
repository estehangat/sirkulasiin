# Design System Documentation

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our user interface.

## Theme Overview
The design system operates in a `light` color mode, ensuring optimal readability and a fresh aesthetic.

## Color Palette

Our brand colors are carefully selected to create a distinct and cohesive visual identity.

* **Primary Color:** `#27AE60` - A vibrant green, used for primary actions, important highlights, and brand elements.
* **Secondary Color:** `#A89F8D` - A sophisticated taupe, offering support for secondary actions and complementary UI components.
* **Tertiary Color:** `#829E60` - A muted sage green, providing an additional accent for badges, indicators, and decorative touches.
* **Neutral Color:** `#F4F4F0` - A soft off-white, serving as the foundational background for surfaces and general content areas.

## Typography

We employ a consistent typographic system to ensure clarity and hierarchy across all content.

* **Headlines:** `plusJakartaSans` - Used for all major headings and titles, providing a modern and clean aesthetic.
* **Body Text:** `plusJakartaSans` - Applied to all paragraph text and general content, ensuring readability.
* **Labels:** `plusJakartaSans` - Utilized for form labels, buttons, and other interactive elements, maintaining consistency.

## Shape and Form

The visual characteristics of our UI components are defined by their shape.

* **Roundedness:** `3` (Maximum, pill-shaped) - All interactive elements and containers feature a highly rounded, almost pill-like corner treatment, creating a soft and approachable feel.

## Spacing

Our spacing system ensures a harmonious and organized layout.

* **Spacing Level:** `2` (Normal) - A balanced approach to whitespace, providing adequate separation between elements for clarity without feeling overly sparse or dense.
